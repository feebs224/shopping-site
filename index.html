How It Works

Get a customized wardrobe in 3 simple steps

01

Personalize Your Style Profile

Begin by creating a style profile on our website. Answer questions about your style, size, and budget to help us understand your preferences. Feel free to upload a few photos or describe your body shape!

02

Let Us Curate Your Wardrobe

Our algorithms will curate a personalized wardrobe for you based on your profile. Explore the recommended items and add your favorites to your cart.

03

Try On and Purchase

You can virtually try on clothes or find nearby stores to try them in person. Once you've made your selections, proceed to checkout, and track your order's delivery. Enjoy your curated wardrobe tailored to your style.
